Account & Data Deletion Request

We respect your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by following the steps below.

⚠️ Please read before proceeding: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your order history, profile information, and saved preferences will be removed.

How to Request Account Deletion

1
Send an email to our support team at utsavmilkproducts@gmail.com using the button below, or from the email address linked to your account.
2
Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered phone number in the message body.
3
We will verify your identity and process the request within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation once deletion is complete.
✉️  Request Account Deletion

What Data Gets Deleted

Data Type Action
Name, phone number, profile photo Permanently deleted
Delivery address & location data Permanently deleted
Account login credentials (Firebase Auth) Permanently deleted
Saved preferences & app settings Permanently deleted
Order history & transaction records Retained for 90 days (legal & accounting requirements)
Payment records (Razorpay) Retained as required by financial regulations

Deletion Timeline

  • Requests are processed within 7 business days
  • You will receive an email confirmation once your data has been deleted
  • Some data may be retained for up to 90 days for legal and financial compliance before being permanently purged
