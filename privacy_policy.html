Privacy Policy

Welcome to Utsav Dairy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our dairy products delivery application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number (collected during account registration via Firebase Authentication)
  • Delivery address and location data (used to process and deliver your orders)
  • Profile photo (if you choose to upload one via the image picker)

Order & Transaction Information

  • Order history, product preferences, and delivery schedules
  • Payment transaction details processed through Razorpay (we do not store raw card or banking details — these are handled securely by Razorpay)

Device & Usage Information

  • Device identifiers, crash logs, and performance data (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Push notification tokens (via Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • App usage analytics to improve app performance

Location Information

  • Your precise or approximate location is collected (using Geolocator and Geocoding) to determine your delivery area and show relevant products and schedules
  • Location is only accessed when the app is in use and with your permission

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your dairy product orders and deliveries
  • To authenticate your identity and maintain account security
  • To send order confirmations, delivery updates, and promotional notifications
  • To generate and share PDF invoices for your purchases
  • To detect and resolve app crashes and technical issues
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To process payments securely via Razorpay
  • To print order receipts on Bluetooth thermal printers (if used by delivery staff)

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services which may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) – Authentication, Cloud Firestore database, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, Push Notifications (FCM), Crashlytics, and App Check
  • Razorpay – Secure payment processing
  • Google Geocoding API – Converting location coordinates to readable addresses

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest
  • We use Firebase App Check to protect our backend from unauthorized access
  • Local preferences and settings are stored on your device using GetStorage
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties

5. Permissions We Request

  • Location – To determine your delivery address and service area
  • Camera / Photo Library – To upload your profile photo or product images
  • Notifications – To send you order updates and delivery alerts
  • Bluetooth – To connect to thermal printers for order receipts (delivery staff only)
  • Internet – Required for all core app functionality

All permissions are requested only when needed and can be managed through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Order records may be retained for legal and accounting purposes as required by applicable law.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data stored in your account
  • Update or correct your personal information at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Opt out of promotional notifications through the app settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
